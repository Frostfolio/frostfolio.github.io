About Me

The name is Eden Angela Edep, a photography hobbyist, and also the owner of this studio. As a college student, I get busy all the time. Taking good photos in my free time, whether it is a scenery, a potrait, or just anything in general is something that satisfies my brain. Think of it as my go to hobby whenever I feel something or my emotions urge me. I think it is special take photos from time to time. Use it as a medium of expression when words aren't enough. As they say, "A picture is worth a thousand words".
